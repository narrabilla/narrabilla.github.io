About

Nice to meet you.

My voice is naturally warm and conversational — genuine, easy to listen to, and the kind that helps a brand feel approachable rather than scripted. I'm comfortable across commercials, narration, e-learning and more, and I happily take on new styles and briefs of all kinds.

I work comfortably in both Indonesian and English (and yes, my English is solid), so I can voice for local and international-facing projects alike. I'm early in my voice-over journey, which means every project gets my full focus, fresh energy, and real care — have a listen to the samples below and hear for yourself.
